Why We Golf

Your swing funds scholarships for women shaping the future of construction.

Now in its 8th year, the AAWIC Golf Tournament has raised over $750,000 for scholarships and programs supporting women in construction. Athens Area Women in Construction (AAWIC) creates opportunities for young women to thrive in architecture, engineering, construction, and the trades. Through hands-on camps, mentorship, and financial support, AAWIC is building a more inclusive and diverse construction industry‚Äîstarting right here in Athens.
